{% include section-header.html title="Our Ideologies" %}

We Read the Bible

It is God’s inspired, inerrant and powerful written revelation to humanity. The Bible is an understandable book that reveals who God is and how people are to live in response to Him.

We Value Community

The church is not a building. It is people who do life together.

We Worship

Worship is more than singing: it is our response to biblical revelation. We vary our worship to include elements that help us integrate what we learn.

We Value Relationships

We encourage people to be real with God and each other.

We Strive for Holiness

While we recognize our shortcomings, we strive, with God’s Spirit, to be transformed into the image of Christ.

We Care

Jesus calls us to be salt and light: to help our community and share the gospel.

We Are Called to Serve

We express our love for each other in word and deed.

{% include section-header.html title="Our Primary Ministries" %}

Worship Services

We provide a highly relational and refreshingly interactive environment that encourages people to engage with God, Scripture, and others.

Wednesday Evening

We develop meaningful relationships as we help each other apply God’s Word to our lives. Classes for all ages.

Community Impact

We see the spiritual, physical and emotional needs of people in our community and seek to meet these needs.

Education

We value the opportunity to offer children a highly effective education. We are closely connected to The JOY Homeschool Co-op which meets in our building each Friday.

The Iron Shop

is CCC’s Manstuff Community Outreach. On Saturday nights throughout the year, we open the doors to families to join us for some super fun indoor activities including a putt-putt course, human foosball, GAGA Ball, an indoor BB gun range, food and more.
